Who I am

I'm a DevOps Engineer at CodetAI, an AI startup based in Karachi, Pakistan. My job is keeping cloud-native infrastructure reliable, observable, and fast — so the engineering team can ship without thinking about the platform underneath.

I hold a degree from IBA Karachi and moved fully into the infrastructure space after a DevOps internship at Systems Limited. Since then I've been deep in GKE, Terraform, and production-grade observability with Prometheus + Grafana.

A big part of my current work involves event-driven data pipelines — streaming real-time database changes using Debezium CDC into Google Pub/Sub, and administering distributed YugabyteDB clusters at scale.
